recipe for Walnut and Blueberry Bran Pancakes

1 1/2 cups whole milk
1 cup instant oats
3/4 cup sifted all-purpose flour (or a blend of white and whole-wheat flours)
3/4 cup blueberries
1/2 cup chopped walnuts
1/4 cup oat flour or oat bran
1 tablespoon baking powder
2 tablespoons honey
1 teaspoon salt
2 eggs, beaten

Pour milk over oats and sift together flour, baking powder, and salt. 
Lightly stir eggs into oats mixture. Add dry ingredients and honey, stirring
until combined. When the batter is thoroughly mixed, stir in the blueberries
and walnuts. Ladle batches of the batter onto a preheated greased or nonstick
griddle or frying pan and cook until tops are bubbly and edges look cooked.
Turn over and finish cooking the other side. Makes about 10 pancakes.
Serves four.

Calories: 400
Fat: 16 g
Carbs: 52 g
Protein: 15 g
